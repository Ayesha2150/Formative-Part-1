About Us

The SPCA Cape of Good Hope is the oldest animal welfare organisation in South Africa, dedicated to preventing cruelty, rescuing animals, and finding them loving homes.

We believe every animal deserves compassion, care, and respect.

Our Mission

To protect all animals from cruelty, neglect, and abuse, and to promote responsible pet ownership.
